List of game States
1. titleState -- tilte screen 
2. playState  -- playing the game
3. pauseState -- paused
4. dialogueState  -- any dialogue
5. characterState  -- player stats and equipment screen
6. optionsState  -- sound settings quiting game etc.
7. gameOverState -- retry or quit
8. tradeState  -- in merchant wcreen
9. mapState  -- diplay map
